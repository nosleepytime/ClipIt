About ClipIt

ClipIt is a smart clipboard manager that allows you to save and organize everything you copy.


How to use:

  • Click the + button to add new clips
  • Use the paste icon to quickly paste from your clipboard
  • Select multiple clips with the trash or share buttons
  • Share your clips as JSON with the share button

Your clips are securely stored in your account and accessible from any device.
